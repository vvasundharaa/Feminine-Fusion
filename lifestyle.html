Lifestyle Tips for PCOS

Regular Exercise

Engage in regular physical activity such as walking, jogging, or yoga to help manage PCOS symptoms.

Healthy Diet

Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins to support hormone balance.

Quality Sleep

Prioritize getting enough sleep each night to support hormone regulation and overall well-being.

Limit Alcohol

Avoid excessive alcohol consumption, as it can disrupt hormone levels and worsen PCOS symptoms.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water throughout the day to help flush toxins from your body and support overall health.

Avoid Smoking

Avoid smoking and exposure to secondhand smoke, as it can worsen hormone imbalances and increase health risks.

Manage Stress

Practice stress-reducing techniques such as meditation, yoga, or deep breathing to help manage PCOS symptoms.

Vitamin D

Get adequate sun exposure or consider taking a vitamin D supplement to support hormone balance and overall health.

Healthy Relationships

Nurture positive relationships with friends and family to promote emotional well-being and reduce stress.
